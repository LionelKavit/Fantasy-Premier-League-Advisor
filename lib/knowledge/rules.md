# FPL Rules — current mechanics (reference)

Rules current as of the **2025-26** season. These are the authoritative mechanics for
squad building, scoring, transfers, and chips — answer rule questions from here, not from
memory, and revise this file when FPL changes the rules between seasons.

## Squad & team
- A squad is **15 players**: **2 GK, 5 DEF, 5 MID, 3 FWD**.
- Budget **£100.0m**; at most **3 players from any one club**.
- Each gameweek you pick a **starting XI** (the other 4 are the bench). Legal formations need exactly **1 GK, at least 3 DEF, and at least 1 FWD** (e.g. 3-4-3, 3-5-2, 4-4-2, 4-3-3, 4-5-1, 5-3-2, 5-4-1).
- **Captain** scores **double**; if the captain doesn't play, the **vice-captain** is captained automatically. Bench players auto-sub in (in priority order) for starters who don't play, if the formation stays legal.

## Scoring
- **Minutes:** 1 pt for playing up to 60 minutes; **2 pts** for 60+ minutes.
- **Goals:** GK/DEF **6**, MID **5**, FWD **4**.
- **Assists:** **3** pts (any position).
- **Clean sheet (60+ mins):** GK/DEF **4**, MID **1**, FWD 0.
- **Goalkeeper saves:** 1 pt per **3 saves**. **Penalty save:** 5 pts.
- **Goals conceded (GK/DEF):** −1 per **2 conceded**.
- **Defensive contribution (added 2025-26):** **2 pts** for hitting a per-match threshold of defensive actions — **defenders at 10+** (clearances, blocks, interceptions, tackles) and **midfielders/forwards at 12+** (those plus ball recoveries). This is the newest rule and the one most likely to be misremembered — apply it.
- **Penalty miss:** −2. **Yellow card:** −1. **Red card:** −3. **Own goal:** −2.
- **Bonus:** the top performers by **BPS** (Bonus Points System) in each match get **3 / 2 / 1** bonus points.

## Transfers
- **1 free transfer** per gameweek. Unused free transfers **roll over**, banked up to a maximum of **5**.
- Each transfer beyond your free ones costs **−4 points** ("a hit").
- Player **prices** rise and fall over the season based on net transfers in/out; you sell at your purchase price plus half of any rise (rounded down).

## Chips
The season is split into two halves and each chip is available **once per half** (GW1–19, then GW20–38) — so two of each across the year. **First-half chips expire at the GW19 deadline** if unused. You may play **only one chip per gameweek**.
- **Wildcard** — unlimited transfers that gameweek at **no points cost** (your squad changes stick). No bench-points effect.
- **Free Hit** — unlimited transfers for **one gameweek only**; the squad **reverts** to its previous state afterward. Good for navigating a blank/double gameweek.
- **Bench Boost** — your **bench players' points count** for that gameweek.
- **Triple Captain** — your captain scores **triple** instead of double for that gameweek.

## Season structure
- **38 gameweeks**. Each has a **deadline** (usually ~90 minutes before the first kickoff) after which the team is locked.
- **Double gameweeks (DGW):** some teams play twice in a gameweek; **blank gameweeks (BGW):** some teams don't play. These drive chip timing (Bench Boost/Triple Captain in DGWs, Free Hit in BGWs).
